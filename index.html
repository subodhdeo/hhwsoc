Few Words About Us

"Helping Hands" is group of peoples who wanted to do something productive towards society. We started this group with motto 'Make A Difference' in life of peoples who need us. Hope we will succeed in this process to bring smile on someone face. Its just a start for us so those who are willing to help but not getting right medium or direction can join us.
